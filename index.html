SCHOOL OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The School of Technology is the oldest department at Bluecrest established in 2010 and employing an outstanding team of international faculty. With up-to-date curricula in an ever-evolving digital landscape, their mission is to ingrain a sustained societal impact through Information Technology education and research. The Information Technology department is equiped with high state of the art computers and other sophisticated machinery that facilitates effective tution.

SCHOOL OF FASHION AND DESIGN

The School of Fashion & Design at Bluecrest was established in 2014 to address the growing demand for garment construction, fashion illustration, digital and graphic design and brand management. Since its inception, it has blossomed into the premier institution in Ghana for budding fashion designers, artists and creative types. BSc. Fashion Design is a 4-years full-time, university-affiliated degree programme that aims to create a responsible designer who will serve the country and society by innovation, education, technology and research. Students will be expected to showcase their collection of designs at fashion shows organised by the college at the end of each semester. This degree is affiliated to the University of Education, Winneba.

The School of Technology is the oldest department at Bluecrest established in 2010 and employing an outstanding team of international faculty.
